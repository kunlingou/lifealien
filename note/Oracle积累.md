### 聚合函数-分组排序取首行

```sql
SELECT * FROM (SELECT CARDRECID,BILLDEFINEID,ROW_NUMBER() OVER(PARTITION BY CARDRECID ORDER BY BILLTIME DESC) RN FROM GAMS_ASSETBUSTRACE) T WHERE T.RN<2
```
